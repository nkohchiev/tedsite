Develop opportunities to suggest ideas and provide feedback

Make sure you build in feedback mechanisms so that the team can generate ideas and solutions.

Staff Survey Manager toolkit

Make use of the staff survey to have conversations with the team about what changes you can make to improve practice or develop staff and identify your reasons to be proud

Our Change

A tool that brings the team together to discuss issues, ideas and solutions. The focus is on the team generating their own ideas and agreeing their own actions to make improvements

The Perfect Shift

A tool that helps you to think about improving how things are done and it could support improving communication within your team

You said, We did

Close the loop with staff by communicating what is happening as a result of their feedback, click here for a “You said, We Did” poster template

Fix it Friday


A tool that helps the team to identify improvements

More Top Tips

Ideas book

Why not set up a book within the work area where the team can log ideas? Make sure to review this and feedback to the team. It may be helpful to invite the team to log issues and also their ideas for improving the issue.

Noticeboard

Don't forget noticeboards are quick and simple ways to share information.
