Why is it important

1.

When everyone in a group thoroughly understands and shares the same vision and purpose they become motivated and highly productive.

2.

When a team has developed a vision, they more easily agree on the important things that they need o work on to achieve it.

3.

It helps set team and individual objectives or goals

4.

People who work toward a future that they want to build are more motivated and productive than those who work only to please the boss.

5.

When the purpose is effective and strong, people get caught up in what they are doing, they become absorbed in their purpose and commit themselves to achieving the team’s purpose – they are engaged.
